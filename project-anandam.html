✦ About Us

Welcome to Anandam

Operation Smile works to provide children with education 📚, healthcare 🏥, and care. We believe every child deserves a safe space to learn and grow 🌱, while building a brighter future for communities.

Project Anandam (Yoga and Art & Craft) 🧘🎨 brings joy and balance to children's lives. Yoga 🧘‍♀️ builds strength and mindfulness, while art & craft ✂️🖌️ spark creativity and confidence 🌟.

Hope for Tomorrow inspires young minds to dream big 💭 and achieve more. Through guidance and skill development 🚀, we nurture confidence and a spirit of giving back 🤝.
